The Reception

Clapton Country Club

Where

The ceremony will be held at Christ Church, Highbury at 1pm.

Please be seated by 12.45pm.

Evening guests are very welcome to join us at the church for the ceremony.

Getting here

Unfortunately parking is for residents only. It will be very busy as Arsenal are playing at home on the day! We strongly advise you to use public transport.

Highbury and Islington station (Victoria Line, Overground and National Rail) is an 8 minute walk from the church.

The church is also on bus routes 4 and 19 and is a short taxi ride away from Kings Cross St Pancras and Euston.
